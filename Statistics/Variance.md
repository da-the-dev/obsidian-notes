How much each point varies from the [[mean]]. Describes the variability in the [[Probability distribution]]
# Discrete case
$$
\sigma^{2} = E[(X-\mu)^{2}]=\sum_{x}(x-\mu)^{2}f(x)
$$
# Continuous case
$$
\sigma^{2} = E[(X-\mu)^{2}]=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} (x-\mu)^{2}f(x) \, dx 
$$
where $\mu$ is the [[mean]].
